How to use

1. Upload Excel
Open 📄 Excel Upload → choose timetable → press Load Excel.

2. Check payday
App automatically shows next payday.
If payday is affected by a bank holiday, it will be shown under the payday.

3. Add shifts
Enter shifts + optional extra hours → press Add.

4. Pay calculation
Pay = shifts × hours × (base + holiday)

5. Bank holidays
Automatically adjusts payday to last working day.
